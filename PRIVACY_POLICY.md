# Privacy Policy for HelloGerman

**Last updated: January 2024**

## Introduction

HelloGerman ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our HelloGerman mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Learning Progress**: Your lesson completion status, scores, and learning preferences
- **App Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, and session duration
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version

### Speech Recognition Data
- **Audio Input**: When using speech recognition features, audio is processed locally and sent to Google's speech recognition service
- **Transcription Results**: Text transcriptions of your speech for learning purposes
- **No Audio Storage**: We do not store or record your voice permanently

### Ad-Related Data
- **AdMob Integration**: Google AdMob may collect data for ad personalization
- **Advertising ID**: Device advertising identifier for ad delivery

## How We Use Your Information

### Learning Features
- **Progress Tracking**: Save your learning progress and achievements
- **Personalization**: Adapt content based on your skill level and preferences
- **Performance Analytics**: Analyze your learning patterns to improve the app

### Speech Recognition
- **Real-time Processing**: Convert speech to text for pronunciation practice
- **Learning Feedback**: Provide feedback on your German pronunciation
- **No Permanent Storage**: Audio is processed in real-time and not stored

### App Improvement
- **Bug Fixes**: Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development**: Understand usage patterns to improve features
- **Performance Optimization**: Optimize app performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Device Storage**: All learning data is stored locally on your device
- **Database**: Room database for lesson progress and user preferences
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not upload your data to external servers

### Data Security
- **Encryption**: Local data is encrypted using Android's built-in security
- **Access Control**: Only the app can access your learning data
- **No Third-Party Access**: We do not share your data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### Google Services
- **Speech Recognition**: Uses Google's SpeechRecognizer API
- **Text-to-Speech**: Uses Android's TTS service
- **AdMob**: Google's advertising platform for app monetization

### Data Processing
- **Google's Privacy Policy**: Speech recognition and TTS are subject to Google's privacy policy
- **AdMob Privacy**: Ad-related data collection follows Google AdMob's privacy policy

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Delete Data**: You can clear app data through Android settings
- **Uninstall**: Removing the app deletes all local data
- **Permissions**: You can revoke microphone and internet permissions

### Opt-Out Options
- **Ad Personalization**: You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings
- **Speech Recognition**: You can disable microphone permissions

## Children's Privacy

### COPPA Compliance
- **Age Restriction**: The app is not intended for children under 13
- **No Child Data**: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- **Parental Control**: Parents should supervise children's app usage

## Data Retention

### Local Data
- **Indefinite Storage**: Data remains on your device until you delete it
- **App Updates**: Data persists through app updates
- **Device Transfer**: Data may be included in device backups

### Speech Data
- **No Retention**: Speech audio is not stored permanently
- **Real-time Processing**: Audio is processed and discarded immediately

## International Data Transfers

### Data Location
- **Local Processing**: All data processing occurs on your device
- **No International Transfer**: We do not transfer data internationally
- **Google Services**: Speech recognition may involve Google's global infrastructure

## Changes to This Policy

### Policy Updates
- **Notification**: We will notify users of significant policy changes
- **App Updates**: Policy changes may be included in app updates
- **Continued Use**: Using the app after changes constitutes acceptance

## Contact Information

### Support
- **Email**: privacy@hellogerman.app
- **In-App Support**: Use the feedback feature in the app
- **Response Time**: We aim to respond within 48 hours

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

### California Residents

## Data Breach Procedures
